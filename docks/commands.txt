These are the available builder commands you can use in the game.
/go x y, moves to a spesified coordinates on the map
/changemap mapname x y, changes your map with a spesified coordinates
/delmap, deletes the map you're currently playing
/reloadmap, reloads the current map
/rawmap, copys the intire data of your map to the clipboard
/gozone, gets the list of available zones that you can go to
/newmap, creates a new map
